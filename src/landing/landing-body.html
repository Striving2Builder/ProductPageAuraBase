🔬 Data Integrity & Accuracy

Our Smart Data Methodology

Most AI trackers rely on LLM estimates, leading to "nutritional hallucinations." AuraBase uses AI for parsing inputs, but anchors all results in peer-reviewed industry databases. We cross-reference your food and supplements with the USDA (United States Department of Agriculture) and the CDI (Canadian Nutrient File) to layer in validated macro, micro, and active supplement compound values with clinical accuracy.

Trust & Privacy

Your data stays yours.

Privacy by design. Not buzzwords — practical protections you can verify.

🔐
PrivateVault Encryption
Uses AES-256-GCM — the global gold standard. Your biometric and personal health logs are encrypted locally before they ever reach the cloud.
🗝️
Zero-Knowledge Protocol
We do not hold your keys. Only you can decrypt your sensitive health history. Not even AuraBase engineers can view your data.
🔒
PBKDF2 Key Derivation
100,000 iterations of key stretching make brute-force attacks computationally infeasible, protecting your credentials even if a hash were ever exposed.
🌐
TLS / SSL in Transit
All data moving between your device and our servers is encrypted end-to-end via TLS/SSL. Nothing travels in plaintext.
🛡️
Row-Level Database Security
RLS policies ensure each user can only ever query their own rows. No cross-account data leakage is architecturally possible.
🔗
Optional Integrations, Zero Defaults
Apple Health, Health Connect, Garmin — you choose what to connect. We display N/A when data isn't available, never fabricate defaults.
🗑️
Full Account Deletion
Delete your account and every associated record from Settings in seconds. Full control, no hoops, no retention.

FAQ

Common questions

What is AuraBase?

AuraBase is a connected health and recovery app that unifies sleep, nutrition, supplements, mood, habits, and training — so you can run personal wellness experiments in The Lab and learn what works for your body.

What is The Lab?

The Lab is AuraBase's personal experiment engine. Pick a change, pick an outcome, check in daily, and see what your own logged data says — like testing whether a walk after dinner improves your sleep score. Read about recovery science →

Does AuraBase replace medical advice?

No. AuraBase is a wellness tool. AI coaches provide informational suggestions only. Consult a healthcare professional for medical decisions.

How accurate is the AI Meal Scanner?

AuraBase uses a hybrid approach: AI identifies food and portions, then anchors nutrition values to official USDA and Canadian Nutrient File databases. Read the technical deep dive →

Who are the AI coaches?

Aria is your International AI Chef and AI Nutritionist. Atlas is your AI Fitness Coach for workout planning and live BioMechanics form analysis. Meet the coaches →

Is my health data private?

Yes. AuraBase uses zero-knowledge encryption for sensitive health logs. You control integrations, and you can delete your account from Settings. Read our Privacy Policy →
